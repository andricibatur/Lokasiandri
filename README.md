## 🇺🇸 English

### 📋 Description
The **Sales Visit App** was born from my real-world struggles as a food business owner (**ZAED**). Managing multiple store deliveries and remembering visit schedules can be overwhelming. This app is **field-tested** by myself to solve those exact productivity gaps.

### 👥 Who is it for?
* **MSME Owners:** Who handle their own distribution and deliveries.
* **Field Sales Agents:** To maintain a simple, digital visit log.
* **Consignment Sellers:** To track goods placed in various retail locations.

### 🛠️ Key Features
* Manage store/partner directories.
* Daily visit logging (dates, times, and notes).
* Delivery status tracking.
* Stock monitoring to prevent out-of-stock issues.

### ⚠️ Technical Note (Data Storage)
This app utilizes **Web LocalStorage**.
* **Data is stored locally on your device (Browser).**
* Please ensure to **Backup your data** (Export) regularly to prevent data loss when clearing browser cache.

<a name="english"></a>
## 🌍 English (International)

### 📋 Project Overview
**ZAED Sales Visit** is a field-tested application designed to solve logistics hurdles for small-to-medium enterprises (MSMEs). Managing last-mile deliveries and tracking store visit cycles is often a chaotic process. This solution provides a structured framework to monitor retail partnerships and inventory flow efficiently.

### 👥 Target Audience
* **Independent Business Owners:** Producers managing their own distribution channels.
* **Field Sales Representatives:** Professionals requiring a streamlined, mobile-first visit logging tool.
* **Consignment Distributors:** Sellers tracking inventory placed across multiple retail outlets.

### 🛠️ Key Specifications
* **Retailer Directory Management:** Organize and manage a comprehensive database of store partners.
* **Dynamic Visit Logs:** Real-time logging of visit timestamps, intervals, and field observations.
* **Last-Mile Inventory Tracking:** Monitor stock levels at each point of sale to optimize replenishment.
* **Delivery Status Monitoring:** Ensure all delivery schedules are met and documented accurately.

### ⚠️ Technical Disclaimer (Data Persistence)
This application utilizes **Web LocalStorage** for data persistence.
* **Data Sovereignty:** All data remains on the user's local device browser, ensuring maximum privacy.
* **Backup Protocol:** Users are strictly advised to perform regular **Data Exports** to prevent loss during browser cache maintenance or system updates.
